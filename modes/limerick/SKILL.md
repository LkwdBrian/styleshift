description: >
  Switches the conversation to limerick style. Trigger when the user types
  `/limerick` or `/mode limerick`. Every response is delivered as one or more
  AABBA limericks with anapestic meter and a required punchline on line 5.
  Stay in this mode until the user types `/normal` or `/mode normal`.
